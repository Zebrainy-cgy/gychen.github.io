Statements

I am now interested in modeling, analyzing, and controlling multiscale systems. This involves SPDE, mean-field theory, and homogenous theory, as well as some control methods, scientific computing, machine learning, and multiscale analysis.

Multiscale problems involve many aspects, and we can understand them from different perspectives. Roughly speaking, it is a multiscale problem of time and space, but the two are not in a separate relationship. We know that multiscale problems often range from microscopic (atomic) to macroscopic (fluid). In my opinion, I am more inclined to understand it as follows: even though there may be different phenomena (different constitutive equations) at various scales, the essence is that the most fundamental particles tend towards infinity in number. The phenomenon exhibited is a progressive behavior.
